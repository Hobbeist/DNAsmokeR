These are the steps to work with this shiny app:

  1. Click on Data upload
    1. Choose the array type that was used to measure your data
    2. Choose the file type that you have saved your data under (either .csv or .rds)
    3. Browse for your data file
    This will load your data frame into the app and automatically populate the dashboard with the following:
    1. The first few lines of your csv fiel and all columns, so you can check if the data was correctly formatted for upload
    2. The prediction results in the form of a confusion matrix, including all relevant model quality parameters
    3. A ROC curve and teh AUC parameter to visualize the quality of the predictor in your data


  2. After that, click on Generate report
    3. This tab will allow you to enter the following details about your study and yourself:
    1. Study name
    2. Normalization method used for your DNA methylation data
    3. A contact email address for your study
  3. Now click Report and a html report of the model measures and all the information required for the validation will be created
